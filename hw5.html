HTML Headers: 1 to 3

How headers are helpful pt.1

Headers are used to help structure and provide headlines in level of importance. It is best practice to have the first biggest headline or title to have the header tag one. Followed by header two, threee, and so on.

How headers are helpful pt.2

To developers, it is important to utilize headers throughout a webpage to highlight what topics or transtitions may occur. For instance, a viewer or user of the website may be searching for the latest recipe for a dish, it may be difficult for them to find it on a webpage with fonts of the same size as nothing seems to standout. With headers, it help guides a reader to see top headlines such as the "Latest Recipes."
