Dragonfly Suite

History of Dragonfly Suite

Started in 1997

Dragonfly Suite was founded by an old senior couple who wanted to leave their mark in their hometown.

Both owners, Jim Henry and Flora Lee decided to
name the suite, Dragonfly after the high school name they met in.

DragonFly Suite Rooms

The DragonFly Suite has three types of room: egg, larva, and adult.

There are tiers of the room. Egg is a single room. Larva is a double bedded room while Adult room is a suite size.
